Location mode
No restriction GPS and all location services work normally at all times. No restriction applied.
GPS off (screen off) GPS satellite chip shuts off when the screen goes off. Wi-Fi and cell-tower location keep working at lower accuracy. Safe for most navigation use cases.
All off (screen off) Every location provider — GPS, Wi-Fi, and cell tower — stops when the screen goes off. Weather widgets, maps, and fitness trackers stop updating in the background.
Foreground only Only the app on screen can access location. Any app running in the background is fully denied, regardless of permissions granted.
Throttle (screen off) Background apps can still get location, but updates are rate-limited when the screen is off. Less battery impact with better app compatibility than a full disable.
